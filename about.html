About Us

Empowering Women, Transforming Futures

Welcome to Enashet CBO, a beacon of hope, empowerment, and transformation nestled in the heart of the Masai Mara region of Kenya. Founded by the visionary Lucy Ketere, Enashet CBO is more than just an organization – it's a community united by a shared vision of empowering girls and women to unlock their fullest potential.

At Enashet CBO, we believe that every girl deserves the opportunity to dream, to learn, and to thrive, regardless of their background or circumstances. Our mission is simple yet profound: to empower girls and women through education, support, and advocacy, paving the way for a brighter, more inclusive future for all.

Led by Lucy Ketere, a trailblazer and the first female guide in Kenya, our dedicated team is committed to making a tangible difference in the lives of those we serve. With unwavering dedication and boundless compassion, we strive to create a supportive environment where young women can flourish, pursue their passions, and become leaders in their communities.

Through our various initiatives, from providing scholarships and educational materials to offering mentorship and inspirational talks, we seek to break down barriers, challenge stereotypes, and foster a culture of empowerment and inclusivity. From the halls of local schools to the hearts of rural communities, our impact knows no bounds, touching the lives of countless individuals and families across the region.

But our work extends far beyond the confines of our programs – it's a testament to the power of collective action and the enduring spirit of hope. Together with our supporters, volunteers, and partners, we are building a movement of change-makers, dreamers, and believers who refuse to accept the status quo and instead strive to create a world where every girl has the opportunity to shine.

We invite you to join us on this incredible journey of empowerment and transformation. Whether through volunteering your time, making a donation, or simply spreading the word, your support can make a world of difference in the lives of those we serve. Together, we can rewrite the narrative, empower the next generation of leaders, and build a future where all girls are free to dream, aspire, and achieve.

Thank you for being a part of the Enashet CBO family. Together, we are empowering women, transforming futures, and building a brighter tomorrow for all.

With gratitude and hope,
